## The "Timing the Market" Myth

Here's the dirty secret Wall Street doesn't want you to know: **nobody times markets consistently.**

The S&P 500 has returned ~10% annually over the last 100 years. But if you missed just the 10 best days in any given decade, your returns get cut in half.

Our approach isn't about timing. It's about:
1. **Systematic entry** — only buy when signals align
2. **Risk management** — 20% stop losses, 25% max position
3. **Continuous learning** — every trade teaches the system

We're building a feedback loop. Win or lose, we get smarter.
