## The Case for Staying Calm

Here's the thing about volatile markets: **they create the best entry opportunities**.

We entered NVDA, TSLA, and AMZN during a choppy period. If the market bounces — and historically, risk-off selloffs driven by geopolitical noise do bounce — we're positioned to capture that upside from our current levels.

Consider:
- **NVDA at $172** is cheaper than it was when institutions were piling in last year
- **TSLA** has massive catalysts ahead (robotaxi, energy, FSD expansion)
- **AMZN** is essentially flat from entry — barely any damage
- **SPY** is the whole market — if the US economy keeps running, SPY follows

We're not underwater on broken theses. We're temporarily underwater on solid companies during a broad macro tantrum. Those are very different things.
